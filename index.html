Why Join INDGO AIR Virtual?

We are more than just a virtual airline; we're a community dedicated to realistic flying, continuous learning, and shared passion.

Experience Realistic Flight Operations

Immerse yourself in true-to-life aviation. We pride ourselves on accurate schedules, modern fleet operations, and an extensive route network that spans from bustling metro hubs like Delhi to scenic regional gems like Kochi.

Thrive in a Vibrant Community

Connect with fellow aviators from across the globe. Participate in weekly group flights, tackle unique challenges, and share your passion in a supportive environment where every pilot is valued, from trainee to seasoned commander.
